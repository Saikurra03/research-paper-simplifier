📖 About This Tool

AI Research Paper Simplifier parses dense academic research papers and generates 25 structured, educational sections using large language models via the Cohere API.

Upload a PDF or paste text - designed for students, researchers, and lifelong learners who want to quickly extract key insights without spending hours reading.

⚙ How It Works

  • Step 1: Upload a PDF or paste the research paper text.
  • Step 2: Choose your target audience level (School Student, College Student, or Researcher).
  • Step 3: Click "Analyze Paper" and wait 15-30 seconds.
  • Step 4: Browse through 25 structured sections with expandable cards.
