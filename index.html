About This Tool

AI Resume Analyzer uses Cohere's Command A model to give you honest, detailed feedback on your resume. It scores your resume, finds missing keywords, checks ATS compatibility, and gives you specific improvements.

Built for job seekers who want real feedback, not just a template.

What You Get

  • Score out of 100 - honest rating of your resume
  • Section-by-section analysis - skills, experience, education, projects
  • ATS compatibility check - will it pass robot screening?
  • Missing keywords - what recruiters are searching for
  • Top 5 improvements - ranked by what matters most
  • Improved examples - rewritten bullet points you can copy

Tech Stack

Python, Flask, Cohere API, PyMuPDF, vanilla HTML/CSS/JS. Hosted on Render.
